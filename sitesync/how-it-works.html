How SiteSync Works

  1. Foreman or on-site Project Manager log daily site activity using a mobile,tablet or laptop/desktop device
  2. Data syncs instantly to the cloud
  3. Managers view progress and safety in real time
  4. Issues are flagged automatically
  5. Reports are generated instantly

This eliminates paperwork, reduces delays and improves site safety and accountability.
